Sauvignon Blanc!

An aromatic white grape variety, Sauvignon Blanc wines are usually high in acidity, and are almost always dry. Great with all kinds of fish!

Eyes, nose, mouth

Wines made from Sauvignon Blanc are usually quite silvery or green in colour, and very opaque. They will give hints of green fruits (gooseberry, green apple) citrus (lemon, grapefruit) and perhaps hints of elderflower and asparagus, on both the nose and the mouth.

You might also like to try Sauvignon Blanc with

This grape is great not only with fish, but also seafood (such as lobster and crab) and white meats, like chicken and other types of poultry. Due to its high level of acidty, Sauvignon Blanc is also used for making sweet wines, and a dessert wine made from the grape pairs well with creamy deserts such as creme caramel, and fruit based deserts, like apple crumble.
