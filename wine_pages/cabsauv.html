Cabernet Sauvignon!

Cabernet Sauvignon wines are bold, sassy and confident. They're deeply coloured, with lots of acidity and strong aromas. A great pairing with any beef dish, especially steak!

Eyes, nose, mouth

Wines made from Cabernet Sauvignon are quite dark and rich in colour, making them hard to see through. They are normally dark red or purpley. You can expect to smell of blackcurrants and black cherry of or maybe even bell peppers and mint! And on the mouth it should taste quite high in acid and tannins, with hints of liquorice, black fruits and perhaps some oaky flavours, such as vanilla and coffee.
